Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 years are curious, imaginative, and rapidly developing. This stage focuses on fostering social, emotional, and cognitive skills through play, exploration, and guided learning experiences in nurturing environments.

Elementary School

Elementary school builds foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and critical thinking. It nurtures social, emotional, and academic growth, fostering curiosity, creativity, and collaboration in a supportive, structured learning environment.

Middle (10-16) Years
